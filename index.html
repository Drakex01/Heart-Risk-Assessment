An innovative approach to predicting 10-year coronary heart disease risk using the Framingham Heart Study dataset

About the Project

This project utilizes machine learning to predict an individual's risk of developing coronary heart disease (CHD) within the next 10 years based on various health metrics. The model was trained on the Framingham Heart Study dataset, one of the most influential studies in cardiovascular disease research.

Our logistic regression model analyzes six key risk factors to generate a personalized risk assessment:

  • Age
  • Sex
  • Cigarettes Per Day
  • Total Cholesterol
  • Systolic Blood Pressure
  • Glucose Level
